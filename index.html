I am a final year undergraduate student pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. Through my academic journey and hands-on experiences, I have developed a keen passion for machine learning, particularly in the domain of computer vision.
During my academic tenure, I delved into various areas within computer science, but it was the intricate challenges presented by machine learning that captivated me the most. My research interest revolves around machine learning and deep learning, with a specific focus on computer vision. This interest has evolved since my second year, and I have fortified my knowledge through rigorous coursework, including artificial intelligence, computer vision, and data mining, undertaken during my current 7th semester.

Experience

  • Summer Intern at IIIT Allahabad (June 2022 - August 2022)
    Worked on the problem related to the diagnosis of autism spectrum disorder using eye-tracking data. Implemented the code of the eye-tracking system using TensorFlow and OpenCV. Also experimented with different CNN and LSTM models for the classification of ASD and non-ASD using eye-tracking data.
  • Research Intern at MixORG (September 2022 - March 2023)
    Worked on the problem related to the selection of human blastocyst (developed stage of embryo) for implantation with deep learning. Implemented the code for generating the next frames of different cell cleavage stages in embryo development videos in Tensorflow using cycleGAN and ConvLSTM model.
  • Research Intern at University of Bonn (June 2023 - June 2023)
    Implemented the code for finding the human pose motion pattern in the video data where human motion data was given in inertial measurement unit (IMU) data using KD Tree and the Python library scikit-learn.
